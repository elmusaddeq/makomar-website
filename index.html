Unified Mission

Who We Are

A Movement Born From Unity

Makomar Jihar Kano is a non-partisan youth political movement dedicated to uniting the young people of Kano State under one powerful, forward-looking agenda — building leadership that serves, not exploits.

🦅
Our Vision

A Kano State governed by visionary, accountable, and compassionate young leaders who put people above politics — where every youth has a voice and a future worth fighting for.

🤝
Our Mission

To identify, train, and promote ambitious young politicians across all 44 LGAs of Kano; bridge ethnic and community divides; and build coalitions that transform elections into true mandates for progress.

⚙️
Our Method

Grassroots organising, digital campaigns, civic education, and leadership academies — forging a new generation of public servants equipped with both ambition and accountability.

Our Foundation

The Six Pillars of Our Movement

Every pillar represents a commitment — not a slogan. These are the values that guide every decision, every campaign, and every leader we uplift.

01
Unity Across Communities

We tear down the walls of tribalism, religious divide, and partisan manipulation. One Kano, one purpose. Our strength is our diversity, not our division.

02
Youth-Led Governance

We reject the culture of recycling failed old guard politicians. Kano's future belongs to the generation that will live in it. We demand seats at the table — not crumbs from it.

03
Transparent Leadership

Every candidate we back signs a pledge of accountability. We track promises, we demand results, and we hold power to account — openly and without fear.

04
Economic Empowerment

Policy must translate to jobs, skills, and businesses. We champion economic reforms that give Kano youth the tools to build wealth, not dependency on political patronage.

05
Education & Innovation

From Almajiri reform to tech hubs, we push for an education agenda that prepares Kano youth to compete globally while staying rooted in their culture and heritage.

06
Civic Participation

Every voter registration drive, every town hall, every debate — we mobilise. A democracy only works when its people show up. We make showing up a movement.

Our Declaration

We, the youth of Kano State, refuse to inherit a legacy of division. We rise — not against each other — but alongside each other. We do not seek power to hoard it. We seek it to share it. For the streets of Sabon Gari, the markets of Kurmi, the classrooms of every LGA — we are one. We unite to lead. We lead to serve. And we serve so that every child born in Kano knows their future belongs to them.
